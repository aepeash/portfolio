A bit about myself

I am a life long Michigander, with dreams of moving somewhere warm after graduating. If you were to have asked me five years ago where I saw myself in 2020 I would have told you Pharmacy school. But sometimes plans change and college freshmen realize they do not like Biology or Chemistry. Within my first month at Oakland University I switched into the Business school wanting to pursue a degree in Management Information Systems (MIS). While studying I learned about business and IT, and the importance of linking technology with a clear purpose, or else it is useless.

My ladies and I

Around the age of ten I watched my mother complete her Masters Degree. After seeing this I knew I also wanted to pursue education beyond a Bachelors degree. Since Pharmacy school was out the window, I started looking into programs with a focus on people and technology. Which is how I found the School of Information (SI)

I am a current first-year in SI focused on Human Computer Interaction. Upon graduation I want to pursue a career in product management or project management. I currently serve as the Information Manager for School of Information Masters Association and work part-time at the Ford School as a web design assistant.
