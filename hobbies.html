My Hobbies

When I am not in class, working, or doing homework, I enjoy baking, working out, reading, and socializing

baking

In times of stress I turn to baking as a way to decompress. My love of baking started when I would bake pies on Thanksgiving with my mom. Looking back on it now I value this time as being a way I was able to spend time with my mom and also learn the fundamentals of baking. I started off making chocolate chip cookies, but enjoy baking cakes, tarts, crips and breads. My family and friends are big fans of my cookies, which is great for me, since cookies are a easy gift for a poor grad student to give

working out

Another way I like to spend my time is working out. I was a cross country runner in high school, and thankfully still enjoy running as a form of working out. I normally run in the summers and find another type of exercise in the winter. There are two reasons for this decision. First, I hate the cold, I am in a constant state of cold from mid-October till March. Second, treadmills are the bane of my existence. Spending five minutes on a treadmill feels like a hour to me. This past winter I started going to workout classes at Cityrow in Ann Arbor. These classes are a combination of cardio on a rowing machine and strength training. The best part is I am able to get in a great workout in the span of 50 minutes.

reading

When the semester is not in full swing I enjoy spending my time reading. Normally all my reading for leisure is done in the summer, so my ideal time to read is when it is sunny and 85 outside while sitting on the porch at my parents house. This past summer I read a few books but the one which stuck with me the most was Orange is the new Black by Piper Kerman. First, this book is nothing like the Netflix show, second, this book really built a sense of empathy for me about the difficulties prisoners go through.

socializing

As a half introvert half extrovert, I find joy in spending time with others. Whether it is spending time with close friends personally or getting together with a large group, I try to allocate a few hours a week to time with friends. I mainly find myself going out to eat with others, I find food to be a very important part of building relationships.
